üîß Technical Details

How Spotify Preview URLs Work:

  • Spotify provides 30-second preview URLs for many tracks
  • These are direct MP3 links that can be played in browsers
  • Not all tracks have preview URLs available
  • Preview URLs are meant for discovery, not full playback
  • This is the only way to play audio without redirecting to Spotify
